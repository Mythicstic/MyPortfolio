MY WORK

🎮 VALORANT

"I developed a web application inspired by the popular tactical shooter, VALORANT. The project serves as a comprehensive database featuring three primary modules: Agents, Maps, and Arsenal."

  • Agents: Users can browse the full roster of characters. Clicking a specific agent redirects to a dedicated page detailing their biography and unique abilities.
  • Maps: This section showcases the game's various environments alongside concise descriptions of their layouts.
  • Arsenal: A categorized directory of the game's weaponry. Each entry includes a description and classification by weapon type to help users understand the in-game economy.
